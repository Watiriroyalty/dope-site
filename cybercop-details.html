Background

On July 12th, 2022, I created this newsletter with a specific purpose in mind. The intention was to provide a dedicated space for individuals who were considering a career in penetration testing.

This biweekly newsletter aims to deliver comprehensive information about the tips and tactics required to excel as a penetration tester. It covers topics such as how to get started in this field and everything one needed to know about the road to cyber security.

In summary, this cybersecurity newsletter is an invaluable resource, arming the reader with knowledge and insights necessary to defend against cyber threats. Its impact has been far-reaching, helping folks stay informed, secure, and resilient in an increasingly interconnected world.
