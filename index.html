About

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Mary Kambo, and I am an ethical hacker, writer, and public speaker. As an ethical hacker, I am passionate about using my skills to help individuals and businesses protect themselves from cyber threats. I have honed my expertise through extensive training and real-world experience, and I take pride in staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in the field. In fact, I am proud to say that I am ranked in the top 1% on TryHackMe, a popular platform for cybersecurity enthusiasts.


In addition to my work as an ethical hacker, I am also a writer and public speaker. I believe that sharing knowledge and insights is crucial to advancing the field of cybersecurity, and I enjoy communicating complex concepts in a clear and accessible way. Whether through blog posts, articles, or speaking engagements, I strive to empower others to take control of their digital security.


Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. I look forward to connecting with you and exploring ways we can work together to build a safer and more secure digital world.
