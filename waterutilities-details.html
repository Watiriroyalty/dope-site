Project information

  • Category: Protecting Water Utilities against Cyberthreats
  • Organisation: eITS & WASPA
  • Speaking Date: 02 June, 2023
  • Location: Sarova Stanley - Nairobi

Background

I had the privilege of delivering a speech to an esteemed audience comprised of CEOs and Managing Directors in the water industry. This high-level gathering provided a unique opportunity to address the critical topic of defending water utilities against cyber threats, emphasizing their paramount importance in today's interconnected world.

As I stood before this influential audience, I shared my insights on the ever-increasing risk posed by cyber threats to water infrastructure. I stressed the urgency of implementing robust cybersecurity measures and adopting a proactive stance to safeguard our vital water utilities from malicious actors.
