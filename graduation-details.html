Background

I had the privilege of delivering the mentee speech at the highly anticipated virtual graduation ceremony of the Women in Cyber East Africa program. As a participant in this transformative four-month mentorship program, I had the opportunity to enhance my technical skills, develop essential soft skills, and engage in inspiring fireside chats.

The virtual graduation ceremony served as a platform to celebrate the achievements of all the incredible women who participated in the program. It was a momentous occasion where we reflected on our collective growth, shared inspiring stories, and celebrated the bonds we formed throughout this empowering journey.
